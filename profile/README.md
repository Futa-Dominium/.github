# Futa Dominium [v0.3.4] By AB Software

In a world where futanari exist, society is divided by prejudice and misunderstanding. Meet Luna, a mischievous 18-year-old girl, as she moves to a new city with her stepmother—a city known for its tolerance of futanari. As Luna settles into her new school and begins to make friends, her arrival coincides with a political storm brewing on the horizon.
On one side stand the Democrats, aligned with conservative factions bent on preserving the status quo, perceiving futanari as a disruptive force. On the opposing end, the Republican Party, once traditional but now dominated by futanari advocates, fervently pushing for expanded rights and recognition. Who will prevail in this monumental confrontation?
